Optimism

The most important factor is optimism . One must keep track of the trends and and feel that its going in the right direction .The optimism rating is considerably higher now than it was 10 years ago.Earning trends have somewhat improved. Owners are positive of better business conditions than the last 6 months

Keep Things in Perspective

If you’re still in business now, you have reason to remain optimistic. Things could be worse. During the Great Recession (2008-2010), it’s estimated that 170,000 closed their doors. This is more than the typical 7.5% of businesses that shut each year, regardless of economic conditions. During the pandemic, it’s estimated by the Federal Reserve that 200,000 business “exited” (a polite term for closed down), but this was a smaller percentage of businesses as compared with closures in the Great Recession and the actual number of exits due to COVID-19 was fewer than expected.So,one should not worry too much .

Work on Staying Positive

One must keep an open mind , allow himself/herself to have a bad day . Things happen to disrupt your business and certain affect how you feel. Just allow yourself to feel those things and then move on.One must make sure to make a list of things you can manage . Focus on what is is in our hand and ignore the ones that are out of control.
